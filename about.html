Sanjay Guthula - Digital Marketing Strategist & Automation Expert

About Me

Overview

Hire Me

Sanjay is a results-oriented digital marketing professional with deep expertise in performance marketing, automation systems, SEO/UI strategy, content production, and marketing infrastructure. With a proven track record of building scalable campaigns and product launches, he has helped brands move from zero to multi-million-dollar revenue through intelligent digital execution.

With over 5+ years of experience and leadership at ZeroCode Innovations, Sanjay combines business insight, marketing innovation, and technological finesse. He has directly influenced more than $10M in business growth, scaled creator brands to 1M+ subscribers, launched 1000+ websites, and crafted 500+ podcasts and ad films — all while managing data-backed automation funnels and centralized marketing systems.

Core Competencies

Performance Marketing & Revenue Growth

Generated $6M+ revenue in a single campaign through data-driven digital planning. Built performance funnels with conversion rates exceeding 20%. Crafted GTM strategies for SaaS, healthcare, personal branding, and educational verticals.

SEO & UI Optimization (1000+ Websites)

Deep knowledge of technical SEO, schema structuring, keyword strategy, and backlink development. Provided UI/UX feedback and layout structures focused on increasing ranking & engagement. Maintains SEO health for 15+ live projects.

Automation & CRM Integration

Multi-step email sequencing using tools like Apollo.io, Instantly.ai, and Gmail API. CRM workflow design: lead scoring, contact tagging, cold email engagement, and nurturing. Built omnichannel automations integrating GMB, WhatsApp, and Google Tag Manager.

Social & Video Branding

Managed 100+ social media profiles. Expert in video storytelling, scripting, directing, and editing for brand videos and product demos. Scaled a YouTube channel to 1M+ subscribers & 20M+ views.

Podcasting & Audio Automation

Created 500+ podcasts, edited with tools like Descript and Murf.ai. Built audio knowledge hubs using branded AI-generated voices. Strategized content publishing across Spotify, Apple, and YouTube.

Bootcamps, Training & Community Building

Designed and led ZeroCode bootcamps like “One Day One App” producing 100+ citizen developers. Facilitated low-code/no-code development workshops. Built podcast academies and client training flows.
