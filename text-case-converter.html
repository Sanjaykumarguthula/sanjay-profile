Free Online Text Case Converter

Convert Text to Uppercase, Lowercase, Title Case, and More

Paste your text into the input area and choose a case conversion type. Your converted text will appear in the output area below.

Convert Text Case Instantly

Our online text case converter allows you to easily change the capitalization of your text. Whether you need to format headlines, titles, or just tidy up some text, this tool is here to help.

Supported Conversions

  • UPPER CASE: Converts all letters in your text to uppercase (e.g., "hello world" becomes "HELLO WORLD").
  • lower case: Converts all letters in your text to lowercase (e.g., "Hello World" becomes "hello world").
  • Title Case (Capitalized Case): Capitalizes the first letter of each word, except for certain minor words like "a", "an", "the", "and", "but", "or", "for", "nor", "on", "at", "to", "from", "by", "with" (unless they are the first or last word). (e.g., "hello to the world" becomes "Hello to the World").
  • Sentence case.: Capitalizes only the first letter of the first word of each sentence and proper nouns (proper noun handling might be basic). (e.g., "hello world. how are you?" becomes "Hello world. How are you?").
  • tOGGLE cASE (Invert Case): Switches the case of each letter in your text (e.g., "Hello World" becomes "hELLO wORLD").

How to Use the Text Case Converter

  1. Paste or type your text into the "Your Text" input field.
  2. Click on the button corresponding to the case conversion you want to apply (e.g., UPPER CASE, Title Case).
  3. The converted text will instantly appear in the "Converted Text" output field.
  4. You can then use the "Copy Output" button to copy the result to your clipboard or "Clear Text" to start over.

Minor words (like articles, prepositions, and short conjunctions) are typically not capitalized unless they are the first or last word of the title.
