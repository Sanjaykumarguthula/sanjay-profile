How to Play Dot Gobbler

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin.
  2. Use the Arrow Keys on your keyboard (Up, Down, Left, Right) to guide your character through the maze.
  3. On mobile devices, use the on-screen directional buttons.
  4. Eat all the small dots in the maze to score points and advance (or complete the level).
  5. Avoid colliding with the ghosts! If a ghost catches you, you lose a life.
  6. Eat the larger "Power Pellets" to temporarily turn the ghosts blue. While they are blue, you can eat them for bonus points!
  7. The game ends when you lose all your lives or clear the maze (if single level).

Game Features

  • Classic Pac-Man inspired gameplay.
  • Navigate a maze, collect dots, and avoid ghosts.
  • Power Pellets to turn the tables on the ghosts.
  • Score and life tracking.

Frequently Asked Questions (Dot Gobbler)

Use the Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) on your keyboard. On mobile devices, on-screen directional buttons will appear. Your character will continue moving in the chosen direction until it hits a wall or you change direction.

Eating a Power Pellet will temporarily make the ghosts vulnerable. They will usually turn blue and flee from you. During this time, you can eat the ghosts for extra bonus points! Be quick, as the effect wears off.

This simplified version, "Dot Gobbler," will feature 2 to 3 ghosts with basic movement patterns to provide a fun challenge.
