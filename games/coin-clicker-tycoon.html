How to Play Coin Clicker Tycoon

  1. Start Clicking: Click the large "Click for Coins!" button to earn your first coins. Each click earns you a base amount.
  2. Buy Upgrades: As you accumulate coins, you can purchase upgrades from the "Upgrades Store". There are two main types of upgrades:
    • Click Upgrades: These increase the number of coins you earn per click.
    • Automatic Upgrades (Generators): These automatically generate coins for you every second, even when you're not clicking.
  3. Increase Your Income: Purchase more upgrades and more powerful versions of them to significantly boost your coins per click (CPC) and coins per second (CPS).
  4. Grow Your Empire: Watch your coin count soar as you strategically invest in upgrades. The goal is to earn as many coins as possible and become a true Coin Clicker Tycoon!
  5. Save/Load Progress: Use the "Save Progress" button to store your game state in your browser's local storage. Use "Load Progress" to resume. "Reset Game" will start you over from scratch.

Game Features

  • Classic addictive clicker/idle game mechanics.
  • Manual coin generation through clicking.
  • Automated coin generation through purchasable upgrades.
  • Multiple types of upgrades that increase click power and idle income.
  • Dynamic display of coin count, coins per click (CPC), and coins per second (CPS).
  • Ability to save and load game progress using browser local storage.
  • Simple, satisfying gameplay loop.
