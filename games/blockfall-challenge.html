Controls

Keyboard:

  • Left/Right Arrows: Move piece
  • Up Arrow / X: Rotate Clockwise
  • Z / Ctrl: Rotate Counter-Clockwise
  • Down Arrow: Soft Drop
  • Spacebar: Hard Drop
  • C: Hold Piece (Optional)
  • P: Pause/Resume

Touch (Mobile): On-screen buttons will appear.



How to Play BlockFall Challenge

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin. New blocks (tetrominoes) will start falling from the top.
  2. Use the Arrow Keys to move the falling block left or right.
  3. Press the Up Arrow (or X key) to rotate the block clockwise. Use Z or Ctrl for counter-clockwise rotation.
  4. Press the Down Arrow to make the block fall faster (soft drop).
  5. Press the Spacebar to make the block drop instantly to the bottom (hard drop).
  6. Complete horizontal lines with no gaps to clear them from the board and score points.
  7. Clearing multiple lines at once (e.g., 2, 3, or 4 lines - a "BlockFall" or "Tetris") scores significantly more points.
  8. The game ends if the blocks stack up to the top of the playing field.
  9. The game speeds up as you clear more lines and advance levels.
  10. (Optional) Use the 'C' key to swap the current piece with a 'Held' piece. You can only hold one piece at a time.

Game Features

  • Classic addictive falling block puzzle gameplay.
  • Seven different tetromino shapes.
  • Smooth controls for piece manipulation (move, rotate, soft/hard drop).
  • Scoring system that rewards multi-line clears.
  • Increasing levels and game speed for a progressive challenge.
  • "Next Piece" preview to help you plan ahead.
  • (Optional) "Hold Piece" functionality for strategic play.

Tips for High Scores in BlockFall Challenge

  • Plan Ahead: Always look at the "Next Piece" preview to decide where your current piece should go.
  • Build Flat: Try to keep your stack as flat as possible, avoiding holes.
  • Aim for Tetrises: Clearing four lines at once (a "Tetris" or "BlockFall") gives the most points. Try to leave a single column open for the I-shape (long bar).
  • Use Soft Drop: Speed up piece descent with soft drop to score more points and play faster, but be careful not to make mistakes.
  • Don't Panic: When the stack gets high or the speed increases, stay calm and focus on making good placements.

Frequently Asked Questions (BlockFall)

Use the Up Arrow key or 'X' key to rotate the current block clockwise. Use the 'Z' key or 'Ctrl' key for counter-clockwise rotation. On mobile, dedicated rotate buttons will be available.

A "BlockFall" (often referred to as a "Tetris" in the original game) is when you clear four horizontal lines simultaneously. This is typically achieved using the long, straight I-shaped block and scores the highest number of points for a single move.

To get a higher score, focus on clearing multiple lines at once, especially four lines (BlockFall/Tetris). Also, try to play at higher levels where the points per line are often increased. Using soft drop can also add to your score. Avoid leaving gaps in your stack.
