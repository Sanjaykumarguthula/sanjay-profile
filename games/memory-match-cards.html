How to Play Memory Match

  1. Select your desired difficulty level. This will determine the number of cards on the board.
  2. Click "New Game" or change the difficulty to start. The cards will be shuffled and laid face down.
  3. Click on a card to flip it over and reveal its image or symbol.
  4. Then, click on a second card to flip it over.
  5. If the two cards match, they will remain face up (or be removed from the board, depending on the game's visual style).
  6. If the two cards do not match, they will flip back face down after a brief moment. Try to remember what was on each card and where it was.
  7. Continue flipping pairs of cards until you have successfully matched all pairs on the board.
  8. The game tracks the number of turns you take. Try to find all pairs in the fewest turns possible!

Game Features

  • Classic concentration card matching gameplay.
  • Multiple difficulty levels to challenge your memory.
  • Tracks the number of turns taken.
  • Clean and engaging card designs (themes can be varied).
  • Simple click-to-play interface, suitable for all ages.
  • Helps improve memory and concentration skills.

Frequently Asked Questions (Memory Game)

You can flip two cards per turn. After flipping the second card, the game will check if they are a match.
