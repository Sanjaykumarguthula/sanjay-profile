BrickBuster Retro - Free Online Breakout Game

Break all the bricks with your paddle and ball. Classic arcade fun!

Controls: Left/Right Arrow Keys or Mouse to move paddle. Click to launch ball.

How to Play BrickBuster Retro

  1. Click "Start Game" to begin. The ball will be attached to your paddle.
  2. Move your paddle left and right using the Arrow Keys or your Mouse.
  3. Click the Left Mouse Button (or press Spacebar - TBD) to launch the ball from the paddle.
  4. The objective is to break all the colored bricks at the top of the screen by hitting them with the ball.
  5. Don't let the ball fall below your paddle, or you will lose a life.
  6. Some bricks may drop power-ups when destroyed. Catch them with your paddle to activate special abilities (e.g., wider paddle, multiple balls, laser shots - features TBD).
  7. Clear all breakable bricks to advance to the next level.
  8. You have a limited number of lives. The game ends when you run out of lives.

Game Features

  • Classic Breakout/Arkanoid style gameplay.
  • Multiple levels with varying brick layouts.
  • Paddle control via keyboard or mouse.
  • Scoring system based on broken bricks.
  • Life system.
  • (Optional) Exciting power-ups to enhance gameplay.
  • Engaging retro sound effects and visuals (to be designed).

Frequently Asked Questions (BrickBuster)

After starting the game, the ball will be attached to your paddle. Click the left mouse button or press the Spacebar (specific control to be finalized during development) to launch the ball upwards.

Power-ups are special items that can drop from broken bricks. Common power-ups might include making your paddle wider, giving you multiple balls, slowing down the ball, or even giving your paddle temporary lasers to break bricks! Specific power-ups will be detailed in the game.

The game is planned to have multiple levels with increasing difficulty and different brick patterns. The exact number of levels will be determined during development.
