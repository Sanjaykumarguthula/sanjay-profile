How to Use Typing Ace

  1. Select your desired test duration (e.g., 30s, 60s, 120s) or choose "Custom Passage" for a specific text.
  2. Click the "Start Test" button. A text passage will appear.
  3. As soon as you start typing in the input area below the passage, the timer will begin.
  4. Type the provided text as accurately and quickly as you can.
  5. The text you type will be compared to the source text. Correctly typed characters/words will be highlighted (or errors will be shown).
  6. Once the timer runs out, or you complete the custom passage, your results – Words Per Minute (WPM), Characters Per Minute (CPM), and Accuracy – will be displayed.
  7. Click "Try Another Passage" or "Start Test" again to practice more.

Features of Our Typing Test

  • Measure your typing speed in Words Per Minute (WPM) and Characters Per Minute (CPM).
  • Calculate your typing accuracy percentage.
  • Multiple test duration options and sample text passages.
  • Real-time feedback as you type (current WPM, errors - TBD).
  • Clear results summary upon completion.
  • Helps improve typing speed and accuracy through practice.
  • Clean and user-friendly interface.

Frequently Asked Questions (Typing Test)

WPM is typically calculated by taking the number of correctly typed words and dividing it by the time taken in minutes. A "word" is often standardized to 5 characters (including spaces). So, `(Total Characters Typed / 5) / Time in Minutes = WPM`. Adjustments for errors might also be included in some calculations.

This version will provide a variety of English text passages. Support for different languages or custom user-provided text could be a feature for future enhancements.

Currently, the test provides results for each session. Saving and tracking progress over time (e.g., using browser local storage to show a graph of your WPM) is a potential feature for future development.
