How to Play Pixel Jumper Adventure

  1. Click "Start Adventure" to begin your journey.
  2. Use the Left and Right Arrow Keys to move your character.
  3. Press the Up Arrow Key or Spacebar to make your character jump. The longer you hold it (up to a limit), the higher the jump might be (feature TBD).
  4. On mobile devices, use the on-screen directional and jump buttons.
  5. Navigate through the level by jumping on platforms and over gaps.
  6. Avoid obstacles and enemies (if any). Touching them might make you lose a life.
  7. (Optional) Collect coins or items scattered throughout the level for extra points.
  8. Reach the designated goal or exit point to complete the level.
  9. You have a limited number of lives. The game ends if you lose all your lives.

Game Features

  • Classic platformer gameplay: run, jump, and explore.
  • Simple and intuitive controls for keyboard and touch.
  • Pixel art style for a retro feel.
  • (To be implemented) Multiple short levels with increasing challenges.
  • Score and life tracking.
  • (Optional) Collectible items and simple enemies.
