How to Play Tap-the-Target

  1. Click the "Start Game" button to begin.
  2. Targets (or "moles") will randomly appear in one of the spots on the game board.
  3. Quickly click or tap on a target as soon as it appears.
  4. Each successful hit earns you points.
  5. Targets will only stay visible for a short period. If you don't hit them in time, they will disappear, and you won't score.
  6. The game typically lasts for a set amount of time (e.g., 60 seconds).
  7. Try to hit as many targets as possible before the time runs out to get the highest score.
  8. (Optional) Some versions might include "bad" targets that deduct points if hit, or power-ups. This version focuses on speed and accuracy.

Game Features

  • Fast-paced Whack-a-Mole style gameplay.
  • Tests reflexes and hand-eye coordination.
  • Score tracking and timed rounds.
  • (Optional) High score saving.
  • Simple click/tap controls, easy to learn.
  • Engaging and fun for quick play sessions.

Frequently Asked Questions (Tap-the-Target)

A standard game round usually lasts for a fixed duration, such as 30 or 60 seconds. The remaining time will be displayed on the screen.

In this basic version, all targets are good and award points. Some advanced Whack-a-Mole games might include "bad" targets (don't hit these!) or targets that give bonus points or time. These features are not in the current core version but could be added.

To get a higher score, you need to be quick and accurate! Try to hit every target that appears as soon as possible. Improving your reaction time and focus will help you maximize your score within the given time limit.
