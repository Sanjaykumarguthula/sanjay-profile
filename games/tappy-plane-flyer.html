How to Play Tappy Plane

  1. Click the "Start Game" button or tap/click anywhere on the game screen to begin.
  2. The plane will start moving forward automatically.
  3. Tap or click anywhere on the game screen (or press the Spacebar or Up Arrow key) to make the plane flap its wings and fly upwards briefly.
  4. Gravity will constantly pull the plane downwards. You need to time your taps to keep the plane airborne.
  5. Navigate the plane through the gaps in the oncoming pipes. Do not hit the pipes, the ground, or the top of the screen.
  6. You score one point for each pair of pipes you successfully pass through.
  7. The game ends if the plane collides with any obstacle.
  8. Try to achieve the highest score possible!

Game Features

  • Simple one-touch / one-click gameplay, inspired by Flappy Bird.
  • Challenging and addictive obstacle avoidance.
  • Score tracking to compete against yourself.
  • (Optional) High score saving using browser's local storage.
  • Clean visuals and smooth animations.
  • Responsive design for play on various devices.

Frequently Asked Questions (Tappy Plane)

Simply tap or click anywhere on the game screen. You can also use the Spacebar or the Up Arrow key on your keyboard. Each tap gives the plane a small upward boost.

The goal is to fly the plane for as long as possible by navigating it through the gaps in the pipes. Each set of pipes you pass successfully earns you a point. The main objective is to achieve the highest score you can.

Tappy Plane is an endless flyer style game. There isn't a predefined end; the challenge comes from trying to beat your own high score and lasting as long as possible against the increasingly tricky (or just relentless) obstacles.
