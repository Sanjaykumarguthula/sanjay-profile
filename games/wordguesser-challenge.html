How to Play WordGuesser (Hangman)

  1. A secret word is chosen, and you see a series of blank spaces representing its letters.
  2. Guess letters one at a time by clicking on the alphabet buttons provided.
  3. If your guessed letter is in the word, all occurrences of that letter will be revealed in the blank spaces.
  4. If your guessed letter is not in the word, a part of the hangman figure will be drawn.
  5. You have a limited number of incorrect guesses (usually 6, corresponding to drawing the full hangman).
  6. The objective is to guess all the letters in the word before the hangman figure is completed.
  7. If you guess the word correctly, you win! If the hangman is fully drawn, you lose, and the word will be revealed.
  8. Click "Get New Word" or "Play New Word" to start a new game.

Game Features

  • Classic Hangman word-guessing gameplay.
  • Visual feedback with the drawing of the hangman figure.
  • Clear display of the hidden word and guessed letters.
  • Interactive alphabet buttons for letter selection.
  • Tracks incorrect guesses.
  • (Optional) Different word categories to choose from.
  • Fun way to test your vocabulary and spelling skills.

Frequently Asked Questions (Hangman)

Typically, you get 6 incorrect guesses before the hangman figure is complete and you lose the game. The number of incorrect guesses remaining is usually displayed.

This version primarily uses a general list of words. Some Hangman games offer categories (like animals, countries, food) to make guessing more specific. This could be a feature added in the future.

Currently, letter guessing is done by clicking the on-screen alphabet buttons. Keyboard input for guessing letters is a potential enhancement for future versions.
