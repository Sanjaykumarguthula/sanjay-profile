How to Play Four-in-a-Row Connect

  1. Select your game mode: "2 Players" to play against a friend on the same device, or "vs Computer" for a single-player challenge. Click "New Game" to start.
  2. The game is played on a 7-column, 6-row vertical grid.
  3. Players take turns dropping one of their colored discs (e.g., Red or Yellow) into one of the seven columns.
  4. To drop a disc, click or tap on the desired column at the top of the board (or on the column itself). The disc will fall to the lowest available empty space in that column.
  5. The objective is to be the first player to get four of your own colored discs in a line â€“ either horizontally, vertically, or diagonally.
  6. If the entire grid is filled with discs and no player has achieved four in a row, the game is a draw.
  7. The current player's turn is indicated on the screen.
