How to Play NumberMerge (2048)

  1. The game is played on a 4x4 grid.
  2. Use your Arrow Keys (Up, Down, Left, Right) on a computer, or Swipe in the desired direction on a touch device to move all tiles on the grid.
  3. When two tiles with the same number touch as a result of a move, they merge into a single tile with the sum of their values (e.g., two '2' tiles become a '4' tile, two '4's become an '8', and so on). The merged tile cannot merge again in the same move.
  4. After each successful move (where at least one tile moves or merges), a new tile – either a '2' or a '4' – will randomly appear in an empty spot on the grid.
  5. The objective is to create a tile with the number 2048.
  6. The game ends when there are no empty spots on the grid and no possible merges (i.e., no adjacent tiles have the same value).
  7. Your score is the sum of the values of all tiles that have been merged.

Frequently Asked Questions (2048)

When you swipe or use arrow keys, all tiles slide as far as possible in that direction. If two tiles with the same number collide during this movement, they merge into one tile showing their sum. For example, if you swipe right and two '8' tiles end up next to each other, they will become a single '16' tile in the rightmost position they can occupy.

The game ends when the grid is completely full of tiles and no more moves can be made – meaning no adjacent tiles (horizontally or vertically) have the same value, so no further merges are possible.

This helps organize the board and set up merges more easily.
