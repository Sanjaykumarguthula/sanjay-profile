Word Counter Features

Our free online word counter provides a comprehensive suite of text analysis tools perfect for writers, students, marketers, and anyone working with text.

  • Comprehensive Text Analysis: Get accurate counts for words, characters (with or without spaces), sentences, and paragraphs.
  • Reading Time Estimator: Understand how long it might take an average reader to go through your text.
  • Keyword Density Checker: Input a specific keyword to see how many times it appears and its density percentage within your text – great for basic SEO checks.
  • Average Word Length: Analyze the complexity of your vocabulary.
  • Easy to Use: Simply paste your text or start typing. Results update in real-time.
  • Clear and Reset: Quickly clear the text area to start fresh.
  • Client-Side Processing: Your text is processed in your browser; nothing is saved on our servers, ensuring your privacy.

How to Use Our Word Counter

  1. Paste your text into the large input area above, or simply start typing.
  2. The counts for words, characters, sentences, and paragraphs, along with reading time and average word length, will update automatically.
  3. To check keyword density, type your desired keyword into the "Keyword" field. The count and density will appear.
  4. Use the "Include Spaces" checkbox to toggle character count behavior.
  5. Click the "Clear Text" button to reset the input field and all counts.

Why Use a Word Counter?

Word counters are invaluable for various tasks:

  • Academic Writing: Meet essay and paper length requirements.
  • Content Creation: Optimize blog posts, articles, and website copy for readability and SEO.
  • Social Media Management: Stay within character limits for platforms like Twitter or LinkedIn.
  • Marketing & Advertising: Craft concise and impactful ad copy or product descriptions.
  • Learning & Editing: Improve writing style by analyzing sentence structure and word choice.
