Generate Your QR Code Instantly

QR (Quick Response) codes are two-dimensional barcodes that can store various types of information. Our free online generator makes it easy to create QR codes for your website, contact details, WiFi access, and much more.

What Can You Use QR Codes For?

  • Website URLs: Direct users to your website or a specific landing page.
  • Contact Information (vCard): Share your contact details seamlessly. (Future feature)
  • Plain Text: Encode messages, notes, or any textual information.
  • Email Addresses: Allow users to quickly send an email.
  • Phone Numbers: Enable one-tap calling.
  • SMS Messages: Pre-fill an SMS message.
  • WiFi Network Access: Share your WiFi credentials easily (SSID, password, encryption type).
  • Event Promotion, Product Packaging, Business Cards, and much more!

Features

  • Multiple Data Types: Supports URLs, text, email, phone, SMS, and WiFi configurations.
  • Real-time Preview (on generate): See your QR code as you customize it.
  • Customization: Change foreground and background colors, and adjust size.
  • Error Correction Levels: Choose from Low, Medium, Quartile, or High error correction to ensure scannability even if the code is partially damaged.
  • Download as PNG/SVG: Get high-quality raster (PNG) or vector (SVG) images suitable for print and web.
  • Client-Side Generation: Fast and secure, all processing happens in your browser.

How to Create a QR Code

  1. Select the type of data you want to encode (e.g., URL, Text, WiFi).
  2. Enter the required information in the fields provided.
  3. Customize the QR code's appearance (colors, size, error correction level) if desired.
  4. Click "Generate QR Code".
  5. Your QR code will appear on the right. You can then download it as a PNG or SVG file.
