Play Pong Classic - Free Online Retro Arcade Game

The original paddle and ball game. Simple, addictive, timeless.

Player 1: 0 Player 2 (AI): 0
Select Mode

1 Player: P1: W (Up), S (Down). AI is P2.

2 Players: P1: W (Up), S (Down). P2: Up Arrow (Up), Down Arrow (Down).

How to Play Pong

  1. Select a game mode: "1 Player (vs AI)" or "2 Players".
  2. The game will start automatically after selecting the mode.
  3. For Player 1 (Left Paddle): Use the 'W' key to move your paddle up and the 'S' key to move it down.
  4. For Player 2 (Right Paddle, in 2 Player mode): Use the 'Up Arrow' key to move the paddle up and the 'Down Arrow' key to move it down. In 1 Player mode, the AI controls the right paddle.
  5. The objective is to hit the ball with your paddle and make your opponent miss.
  6. If your opponent misses the ball, you score a point.
  7. The first player to reach a certain score (e.g., 10 points) wins the game, or play continues.

Game Features

  • Classic Pong gameplay, one of the earliest arcade video games.
  • Single-player mode against a simple AI opponent.
  • Two-player local mode for challenging a friend.
  • Simple, intuitive controls.
  • Real-time score tracking.
  • Clean, retro-inspired visuals.
